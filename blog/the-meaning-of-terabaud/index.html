The meaning of terabaud

terabaud stands for 10^12 baud. A baud is a unit for data transmission bandwidth. I came to this nickname in my youth when I played DOS games with my brother over a serial network connection. A usual connection speed for null modem cable was 9600 baud. It took ages to transmit data from one computer to another and I basically wanted more speed so I prefixed baud with tera.

I decided to keep my nickname for now :)
